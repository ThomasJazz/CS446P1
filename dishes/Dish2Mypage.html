XBurger One X 360

With 40% more power than any other hamburger, experience immersive true 4Kcal eating.



Cost: $3.12
Calories: 570 kcal
Total Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

AMD Wheat Buns x 2
MS Active Beef x 300g
Tomato Slices x 2
Red Onion Slices x 3
Lettuce Leaves x 2
Broccoli x 1
Sauce Of Your Choosing (Ketchup and Mustard Recommended

Directions
  1. Place the burger patty onto the grill.
  2. Lightly grill both buns either on the grill or in a toaster.
  3. Set the buns off to the side once you have the level of crispy bun that you like.
  4. While the burger patty is still getting grilled, drop that piece of broccoli into a blender and blend until it's almost liquid.
  5. Set the broccoli blend off to the side.
  6. Grab the bottom bun of the burger and place both pieces of lettuce on it.
  7. Grab the 3 onion slices and place them on top of the lettuce.
  8. Next, place the 2 tomato slices on top of the onions and lettuce.
  9. Finally, take your burger patty off the grill and place it on top of the tomato's.
  10. Pour the sauce of your choice onto the burger.
  11. Pour the broccoli blend on top of the sauce.
  12. Place the top bun onto the finished burger.
  13. Enjoy!
